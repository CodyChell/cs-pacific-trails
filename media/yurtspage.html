What is a Yurt?

Yurt
A Yurt is round tent covered with skins or felt and used as a dwelling by nomads in the steppes of Central Asia.

What is it made of?

The structure comprises an angled assembly or latticework of pieces of wood or bamboo for walls, a door frame, ribs, and a wheel possibly steam-bent.
